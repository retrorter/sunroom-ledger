### ✂️ Jalapeno Pruning Protocol

When clearing out foliage on the porch-side jalapeno, execute a strict **bottom-pruning strategy**:

* **The 6-Inch Clearance Rule:** Focus exclusively on individual fan leaves along the lowest 6 inches of the main stalk. The objective is to eliminate any low-hanging foliage that can physically contact the soil matrix or catch dirt splash-back during rain loops, removing prime transmission vectors for soil-borne fungal pathogens.
* **Leaves vs. Side Shoots:** Snipping should target *only* the independent, mature leaves. If you notice tiny, new axial side-shoots or nodes branching outward directly above where those lower leaves meet the main stem, leave them alone. Those will eventually grow into structural secondary branches that increase your overall canopy fruit yield.
* **Sanitation:** Give your tools a swift swipe with isopropyl alcohol before starting to guarantee a clean, pathogen-free cut on the main vascular line.
